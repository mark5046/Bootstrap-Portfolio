About Me

I've worked as a firefighter, in EMS, as a personal trainer, as well as a team lead for an opposition force group attached to the U.S.A.F 421st Combat Training Squadron doing pre-deployment training. I enjoy riding motorcycles and relaxing at home binge watching TV shows online.
